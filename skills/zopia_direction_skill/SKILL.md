# Zopia Direction Skill

脚本をもとに、Zopiaで使う英語プロンプト、素材一覧、保存パスを作る。

## 固定キャラ
- Mamoru Moriya: Japanese male office worker, late 30s to early 40s, sincere, tired, slightly troubled, casual office clothes, information systems staff vibe.
- Ritsuko Aizawa: smart Japanese female manager, composed, politically skilled, calm but intimidating.
- Kuma Kacho: large intimidating male manager archetype, heavy presence, office boss energy.
